No problem — email Matt directly and he'll schedule a regular Zoom intake with you instead. Same price, same 48-hour turnaround either way.

Is Annie actually an AI?

Yes — Annie is an AI voice agent. She asks the questions that help us understand your business. Matt personally reviews the transcript and every recommendation in your report before it's sent. No robot signs off on what you get.

What if I don't know anything about AI yet?

Perfect — that's exactly who this is for. You don't need to prepare. We ask about your week, your tools, and your headaches. You don't have to know a single AI tool by name.

Do you do the implementation for me?

Not as part of the assessment — that keeps the scope tight and the price predictable. Most clients install the tools themselves with the step-by-step guidance in the report. If you'd rather we set everything up, we offer an implementation sprint as a separate engagement after the assessment.

What about my data — is the call recorded?

Yes, calls are recorded and securely stored to generate your transcript. We use Retell for the voice agent and Anthropic's Claude for analysis. We delete recordings and transcripts after 90 days and never share your data. Full privacy policy linked in the footer. Questions? Email matt@effortlessflow.ca.
